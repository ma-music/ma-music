Spicy italian sandwich

Ingredients
  • sub roll
  • mayo
  • cheese (traditionally provolone)
  • sliced ham, turkey, salami and pepperoni
  • shredded iceberg lettuce
  • tomato
  • olive oil
  • vinegar
  • italian seasoning
  • salt and pepper ((to taste)

Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Cut sandwich rolls along the seam (do not cut all the way through)
  3. Spread mayo on each side of the sub roll.
  4. Top each roll with cheese, ham, turkey, salami, and pepperoni.
  5. Heat in the oven for 5 minutes or until cheese melts.
  6. In the meantime, chop the lettucea and place in a bowl.Whisk together the oil, vinegar, and seasoning. Pour the dressing over the lettuce.
  7. When sandwiches have been heated, divide the lettuce among each of the subs and then top with some tomato slices.
